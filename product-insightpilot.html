NyLi Insights

NyLi Insights is the analytics and business intelligence component of the NyLi suite. It helps teams turn data into actionable insight with dashboards, AI-generated narratives, and natural-language questions.

Request a demo Login options

How NyLi Insights works (typical flow)

1. Bring data together

Configure data ingestion from relevant sources so key metrics (sales, marketing, engagement, operations) can be analyzed in one place.

2. Build dashboards and reports

Use dashboards and visual reports so teams can review KPIs and trends without stitching spreadsheets manually.

3. Ask questions in plain language

Query data using natural language, like: “How are our sales trending this quarter?” or “Which product is performing best and why?”

4. Get summaries and notifications

Receive AI-generated narrative summaries and alert-style updates when key metrics shift.

Core capabilities

Dashboards and reporting

Track KPIs and trends with visual dashboards and reports designed for routine decision cadence.

Natural-language analytics

Ask questions in plain language and get answers derived from the underlying data.

AI narrative analysis

Generate plain-English summaries that help non-technical users understand what changed and why it matters.

Common use cases

  • Sales pipeline visibility and forecasting prep for review meetings.
  • Marketing campaign effectiveness and ROI conversations.
  • Customer success and retention pattern monitoring.
  • Strategic decision support by combining internal performance and context.

Trust and access

Role-based access

NyLi Insights supports authenticated user access and role-based control so sensitive data is limited to approved users.

Shared context

Dashboards and summaries help teams align on the same numbers, reducing reporting drift and version confusion.
