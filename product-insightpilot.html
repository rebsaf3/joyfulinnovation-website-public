NyLi Insights

NyLi Insights is the analytics layer of the NyLi suite. It helps teams move from raw data to shared understanding and action.

How the workflow runs

1. Bring data together

Connect source systems or upload files so key business metrics are available in one place.

2. Build dashboards and views

Create visual reporting layouts for recurring team and leadership review sessions.

3. Ask questions in plain language

Use natural-language prompts like "Which segment grew fastest this quarter?" to accelerate analysis.

4. Share summaries and alerts

Deliver AI-generated narrative summaries and recurring alert updates so stakeholders can act on changes quickly.

Core capabilities

Interactive dashboards

Track KPIs and trends with visual reporting that supports ongoing decision cycles.

Natural-language analytics

Ask business questions directly and get data-backed answers without writing technical queries.

Narratives and notifications

Use summaries and alert workflows to keep teams informed between formal review meetings.

Typical use cases

  • Quarterly sales pipeline visibility and forecast conversations.
  • Campaign performance review across marketing and business development teams.
  • Customer retention and product usage trend monitoring.
  • Executive briefing summaries from combined operational data sources.

Trust and access

Secure workspace access

NyLi Insights supports authenticated access and role-based controls so sensitive data remains limited to approved users.

Shared decision context

Teams can review the same dashboards and AI summaries, reducing reporting drift across departments and stakeholders.
