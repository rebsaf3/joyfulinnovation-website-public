InsightPilot

InsightPilot turns raw business data into AI powered visualizations and interactive dashboards. Upload CSV, Excel, or JSON, describe what you want in plain English, and get production quality Plotly charts in seconds without writing code.

Product Suite

InsightPilot completes the Joyful Innovation suite with AssetPilot and FlowPilot.

Why Teams Choose InsightPilot

Most organizations wait on analysts for one off requests, and traditional BI tools require too much setup for non technical users. InsightPilot gives teams a faster path from data to decision with plain language prompts, reusable dashboard workflows, and security controls designed for production use.

  • Natural language prompts generate interactive Plotly charts in seconds
  • No SQL, no Python, no drag and drop builder required
  • Business users can self serve insights without waiting for analysts
  • Teams collaborate with role based access in secure multi tenant workspaces
  • Enterprise controls include SSO, 2FA, audit logging, and white label options

Data Pipeline

Upload CSV, Excel, or JSON with smart parsing, encoding detection, and automated column profiling for cleaner analysis inputs.

AI Analysis Flow

Use the three step workflow of Describe, Review, and Save. Revise charts with follow up prompts and auto retry error correction.

Dashboard Delivery

Save multi chart dashboards, manage chart order, and export polished reports for leadership and stakeholders.

Security and Governance

Password auth, Google and Microsoft SSO, SAML, TOTP and email 2FA, backup codes, API key controls, and full admin audit trails.

Architecture

Built on Python, Streamlit, FastAPI, SQLite, and Claude AI with Stripe billing and Railway deployment for fast delivery and durability.

API and Automation

REST API endpoints support programmatic uploads, analysis, and dashboard workflows for internal automation and integrations.

Who InsightPilot Is For

Business and Ops Teams

Managers and analysts who need fast answers from raw exports without waiting on a full analytics queue.

IT and Data Leaders

Teams that need secure access controls, audit visibility, and workspace governance for multi team adoption.

Developers and Integrators

Technical teams that want API driven chart generation and reporting workflows in existing systems.
