How AssetPilot Works

1. Upload and Compare

Upload license and AD CSV files. AssetPilot matches records by email and flags inactive and mismatched users instantly.

2. Verify Automatically

Send tokenized confirmation emails to flagged users. Responses update in real time as pending, confirmed, or revoked.

3. Reclaim and Report

Use dashboard evidence to execute deactivations, plan renewals, and document compliance outcomes.
