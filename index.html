How AssetPilot Works

1. Upload & Compare

Upload license and AD CSV files. AssetPilot matches records by email and highlights inactive and mismatched users instantly.

2. Verify Automatically

Send tokenized confirmation emails to flagged users. Responses are tracked in real time as pending, confirmed, or revoked.

3. Reclaim & Report

Use dashboards and exports to execute deactivations, improve renewal planning, and document compliance outcomes.
