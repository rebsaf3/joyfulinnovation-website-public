Advisory services to reduce manual work and implement AI safely

We help teams reduce manual work, shorten cycle time, and implement AI with practical governance. Our services support NyLi Assets, NyLi Agent, and NyLi Insights rollouts based on your operational priorities.

Workflow Automation Strategy

Map current processes, identify bottlenecks, and design automation paths that reduce handoffs and improve execution continuity, often as part of NyLi Agent initiatives.

AI Use Case Design

Prioritize high-value AI opportunities, define success measures, and build rollout plans that align outcomes across NyLi Assets, NyLi Agent, and NyLi Insights.

AI Governance Implementation

Assess governance posture, define AI control frameworks, and establish clear policy ownership and risk management practices.

Implementation and Enablement

Deliver pilot builds, train teams, and establish operating playbooks for NyLi Assets, NyLi Agent, and NyLi Insights so adoption continues after launch.

How to Pick a Starting Point

  • Start with NyLi Assets when renewal timing, audit readiness, or license waste is the immediate priority.
  • Start with NyLi Agent when work slows around approvals, handoffs, and follow-up execution.
  • Start with NyLi Insights when reporting cycles are slow or leadership visibility is limited.
