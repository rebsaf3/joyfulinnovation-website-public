AssetPilot

AssetPilot finds wasted SaaS spend hiding in plain sight. It compares what you are paying for against who is actually active, verifies ownership automatically, and generates an evidence trail you can take to procurement, security, and audit.

Product Suite

AssetPilot works as part of the Joyful Innovation suite together with FlowPilot and InsightPilot.

Featured Product

Compare, verify, and revoke in one continuous audit cycle

Teams buy AssetPilot when renewals are coming up and spreadsheets are failing them. It reduces manual chasing, limits risky deactivations, and gives procurement defensible data for negotiation.

Upload and Compare

Upload two CSV files, one from license assignments and one from AD or HR exports. AssetPilot matches users by a shared key such as email and flags inactive or mismatched records instantly.

Verify Automatically

For potentially inactive users, AssetPilot sends tokenized confirmation emails and tracks responses in real time with pending, confirmed, and revoked status.

Comparison output

See matched users, users only in licenses, users only in AD, and field conflicts such as department or name mismatches.

Reclaim and report

Use run dashboards, trend charts, CSV export, and chronological activity trails with timestamp and IP logging.

Business outcome

Recover license spend quickly, reduce manual effort, and strengthen compliance readiness.
