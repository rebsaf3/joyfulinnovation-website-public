AssetPilot

A multi-tenant SaaS platform for IT teams to identify inactive software licenses, verify usage automatically, and reclaim spend with compliance-ready evidence.

Featured Product

Compare, verify, and revoke in one continuous audit cycle

Upload & Compare

Upload two CSV files, one from license assignments and one from Active Directory or HR exports. AssetPilot matches users by a shared key such as email and highlights inactive or mismatched records instantly.

Verify Automatically

For potentially inactive users, AssetPilot sends tokenized confirmation emails and tracks responses in real time with pending, confirmed, and revoked statuses.

Comparison output

See matched users, users only in licenses, users only in AD, and field-level conflicts such as department or name mismatches.

Reclaim & report

Use run-level dashboards, trend charts, CSV export, and chronological audit trails with timestamps and IP logging.

Business outcome

Recover license spend quickly, reduce manual spreadsheet work, and strengthen audit/compliance readiness.

Built for Teams That Manage SaaS Spend

IT asset managers

Get a clear picture of active and inactive license ownership across every audit run.

Procurement and finance

Export defensible data for renewal negotiations and cost optimization planning.

Compliance and audit leaders

Access full audit trails for email sends, responses, and status changes per run.

Architecture Highlights

Multi-tenant by default

Company data is isolated with strict company-level scoping for secure tenant separation.

Lightweight stack

Node.js and Express backend, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend, and SQLite in WAL mode.

Fast setup

No deep integration required. Upload CSV files up to 10 MB each and start auditing in seconds.
