NyLi Assets

NyLi Assets is a digital content management platform: a central hub where teams store, organize, and retrieve business assets like decks, proposals, FAQs, reports, and approved messaging.

Request a demo Login options

How NyLi Assets works (typical flow)

1. Upload and organize content

Add documents and media through a web interface. Organize content with categories, descriptions, and tags so teams can browse by product, industry, or content type.

2. Search in plain language

Find what you need without memorizing filenames. Search by meaning and context to surface the right asset even when its name is generic.

3. Use assets during real work

Pull approved content for meetings, proposals, and responses to questions. Teams spend less time hunting and more time executing.

4. Keep versions consistent

Update the asset in one place so teams use the latest approved version and avoid sending outdated specs or slide decks.

Core capabilities

Central hub for business assets

Store decks, brochures, proposals, videos, research, and FAQs in one shared library.

AI-assisted organization and retrieval

Support semantic search and intelligent organization so teams can find content quickly using natural-language queries.

User accounts and permissions

Use logins and role-based permissions so admins can control who can upload, edit, and access content.

Typical use cases

  • Prepare for client meetings by pulling industry-relevant materials quickly.
  • Draft proposals and RFP responses with approved language and templates.
  • Onboard new team members with searchable training and reference content.
  • Maintain brand consistency by keeping approved logos and messaging in one place.

Trust and access

Controlled access

NyLi Assets supports secure authentication and roles so only authorized users can view, upload, or update assets.

Built to complement NyLi Agent

NyLi Assets is designed to complement NyLi Agent by storing and delivering the content and data the assistant and business users might need.
