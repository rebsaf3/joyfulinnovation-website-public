Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about AssetPilot setup, audit workflow, security, and pricing.

How do we get started with AssetPilot?

Start by uploading two CSV files: one with license assignments and one with Active Directory or HR user exports. AssetPilot compares records immediately.

What does AssetPilot flag in comparison?

Matched users, users only in licenses, users only in AD, and field-level conflicts such as different department values or name variants.

How does verification work?

AssetPilot sends tokenized confirmation emails to potentially inactive users. Responses are tracked in real time as pending, confirmed, or revoked with timestamped logs.

What pricing tiers are available?

Starter (Free), Professional ($49/mo), and Enterprise (Custom). See the AssetPilot page for included limits and features by tier.
