Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about setup, security, and reclaim outcomes.

How long does a first run take end to end?

Most teams can run the compare step in minutes once they export two CSV files. Verification runs on your schedule, then the reclaim list updates as confirmations arrive.

What do we need to export from our license system?

A CSV file with user identifier and license assignment is enough to start. More fields improve matching, but AssetPilot can work with a minimal export.

Do you store our CSV data and for how long?

Data handling follows our privacy policy. Retention can vary by plan and configuration. Contact us for guidance on your preferred data handling model. See our Privacy Policy for more detail.

Can we disable verification emails and only use compare?

Yes. Many teams start with compare only to identify obvious waste and validate data quality before turning on verification.

How do you prevent risky deactivations?

AssetPilot provides recommendations, not automatic deactivations. It gives evidence, status tracking, and verification signals so people can make safe final decisions.

What security controls exist for multi tenant separation?

AssetPilot applies tenant isolation at the application level so company data remains scoped and separated. Contact us for a detailed security review for your environment.

Do you support SSO or SCIM?

Yes. SSO support is available for Enterprise deployments, with governance options aligned to larger environments.

What does onboarding look like?

We guide your export setup, run the first compare cycle with you, and provide a walkthrough of verification and reclaim actions so your team can operate confidently.
