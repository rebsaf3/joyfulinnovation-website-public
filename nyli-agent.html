Frequently asked questions

How is NyLi Agent different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

NyLi Agent is a governed business deployment, not a consumer chatbot. It includes account-based access control, integration with your organizational knowledge, usage governance, and an implementation process — not just a chat interface you sign up for.

What kinds of workflows is NyLi Agent best suited for?

NyLi Agent works best for high-frequency, text-heavy tasks: drafting outreach and update emails, summarizing meeting notes or reports, answering internal product and process questions, and supporting onboarding. We scope use cases in discovery to ensure fit.

Does NyLi Agent learn from our company data?

NyLi Agent can be connected to your governed content library through NyLi Assets, which grounds its responses in your actual business materials. This is different from model training — it improves relevance without modifying the underlying AI model.

How do you handle data privacy and security?

NyLi Agent uses authenticated accounts, so only authorized users access the assistant. We work through data handling and access scope during the discovery and deployment process to ensure your organization’s standards are met.

How long does deployment take?

A focused pilot deployment typically runs 3–6 weeks from scoping to first active use. We include enablement, review cadence setup, and a handoff plan so teams can sustain usage independently.

What happens after the initial deployment?

We provide ongoing engagement options to help teams expand use cases, refine governance, and integrate with additional NyLi products as your organization’s AI readiness grows. We do not hand off and disappear after go-live.
