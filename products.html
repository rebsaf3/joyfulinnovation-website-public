NyLi Assets

One sentence: NyLi Assets is a web-based content and knowledge hub where teams store, manage, and retrieve business assets with natural-language search.

Capabilities

  • Central library for decks, proposals, FAQs, research, and other business assets.
  • Search and retrieval in natural language, designed to reduce time spent hunting for the right version.
  • User accounts and permissions so admins can control who can view, upload, and update assets.
