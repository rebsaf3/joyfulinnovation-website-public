Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026. Last updated: February 13, 2026.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Joyful Innovation website, contact forms, communications, and related service interactions. It does not govern third party websites or services that may be linked from this site.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, work email, company name, and message content. We may also collect technical data such as browser type, device information, IP address, and approximate usage data required for security, diagnostics, and site performance.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, communicate about delivery and support, maintain security, improve website performance, and comply with legal obligations. We do not use submitted information for unrelated purposes without an appropriate legal basis.

Legal bases for processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we process personal information based on one or more of the following: your consent, performance of a contract, legitimate business interests, and compliance with legal obligations.

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers that support hosting, analytics, communications, and operational delivery under confidentiality and security obligations. We may also disclose information when required by law, court order, or lawful government request.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions outside your location. When cross border transfers occur, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable data protection law.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal or regulatory obligations. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and service relationship.

Security measures

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be fully secure, and we continuously improve controls as risks evolve.

Your privacy rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of processing of your personal information. You may also request a copy of certain information you submitted. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@joyfulinnovation.ai.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to support essential site functions, analytics, and performance improvements. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some site functionality may be limited if disabled.

Children privacy

Our website and services are intended for business users and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective or last updated date on this page. Continued use of this site after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, requests, or concerns, contact Joyful Innovation at hello@joyfulinnovation.ai.
