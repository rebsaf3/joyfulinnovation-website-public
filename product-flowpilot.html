NyLi Agent

An automation layer that drives operational follow-through, reduces handoffs, and keeps critical workflows moving.

Product Suite

NyLi Assets identifies and verifies what should happen, and NyLi Agent executes the follow-through steps and escalations so work does not stall. NyLi Insights then makes outcomes visible through reporting and dashboards.

NyLi Agent automates operational follow-through across triage, routing, approvals, escalations, and status updates in email, service desk, and collaboration workflows. It supports execution while keeping decision ownership with your team.

  • Auto-route tickets based on keywords and metadata
  • Trigger approvals and log outcomes
  • Generate drafts and summaries for inbox triage workflows
  • Push updates across tools when status changes
  • Escalate when SLAs are at risk

Example: from reclaim candidates to completed action

  • NyLi Assets identifies reclaim candidates and verification status as the operational input.
  • NyLi Agent routes approvals, follows up on pending items, and escalates stalled work.
  • NyLi Insights summarizes cycle outcomes for leadership visibility and renewal planning.

Automation scope

Ticket triage, approvals, follow-up actions, escalations, and cross-tool status execution.

Business value

Reduce manual handoffs, improve workflow continuity, and support more predictable operational delivery.
