NyLi Agent

An automation layer that removes handoffs and keeps critical processes moving.

Product Suite

NyLi Agent is part of the Joyful Innovation suite alongside NyLi Assets and NyLi Insights.

NyLi Agent automates the annoying middle of operations: triage, routing, approvals, escalations, and follow up actions across email, service desk, and collaboration tools. Less chasing. Faster cycle time. Fewer dropped requests.

  • Auto route tickets based on keywords and metadata
  • Trigger approvals and log outcomes
  • Generate drafts and summaries for inbox triage
  • Push updates across tools when status changes
  • Escalate when SLAs are at risk

Automation scope

Ticket triage, escalations, follow up actions, approvals, and cross tool execution.

Business value

Shorter cycle time, fewer manual handoffs, and more predictable delivery quality.
